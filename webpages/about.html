About me

Personal background

As an Indian who had the privilege of being born and raised in Dubai, I was able to experience the best of both worlds. Growing up in the vibrant multicultural environment of Dubai, I developed a profound fascination for technology and engineering. The city's constant evolution and its futuristic skyline provided an inspiring backdrop to nurture my passion for understanding the inner workings of the latest technological advancements. This unique blend of cultures and opportunities greatly influenced my decision to pursue a career in engineering.

My educational journey led me to Middlesex University Dubai, where I earned my degree in Computer Systems Engineering with first-class honors. This university experience not only deepened my knowledge but also instilled in me a sense of purpose and a commitment to excellence in the field. Dubai has been an integral part of my life's story, shaping my aspirations and ambitions, and I am proud to call it my home.

Experience

Empromis Solutions is a leading provider of enterprise software solutions that help organizations of all sizes to increase efficiency and optimize performance .At Empromis Solutions, I was responsible for developing software solutions that helped clients streamline their business processes. I worked on several projects that involved developing custom software applications for clients in various industries.

Flydubai is an Emirati government-owned airline that operates a total of 114 destinations, serving the Middle East, Africa, Asia, and Europe from Dubai . At Flydubai, I was part of the IT team that managed the airline's reservation system. I was responsible for ensuring that the system was running smoothly and that any issues were resolved quickly.

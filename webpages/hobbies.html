F1 racing, the pinnacle of high-speed motorsport, finds its roots in the heart of Europe. This adrenaline-pumping sport has always captivated me, and I have a few favorites that make it even more exciting. Lewis Hamilton, the maestro behind the wheel, has clinched 7 world championships, and he's the one I look up to. Supporting his team, Mercedes AMG Petronas, adds an extra layer of thrill to every race.
